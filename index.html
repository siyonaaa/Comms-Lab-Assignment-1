The NYUAD Campus in Bloom

About the Video

“NYUAD Campus in Bloom” is a short movie about the beautiful flora of our campus. Sometimes flowers are just tiny, but we are mostly too busy to notice the pops of color amongst the shining greens and yellows. Nature and especially color has a huge impact on our moods when we are outside and this university has a wide variety of plants and flowers. This video explores the significance of the colors found in some flowers for students in a university context.
